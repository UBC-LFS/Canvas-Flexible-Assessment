Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change student choices after the deadline?
Under Student Choices, you can click that student's name and there you will be able to input updated choices.
If I disable Flexible Assessment from Canvas will the data inside be lost?
No, disabling Flexible Assessment from Canvas will have no impact on Flexible Assessment data. If you re-enable it, your setup and any student choices will still be there.
Why is the Total grade in Canvas Gradebook different from the Default Total in Flexible Assessment?
Flexible Assessment uses the default assessment weights to calculate the default total. If you are hiding Assignment Group Weights in Canvas, unhide them so Canvas gradebook calculates the total grade properly with the assessment group weights.
