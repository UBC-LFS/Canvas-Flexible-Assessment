Import Assessments

You may import assessment configurations via CSV upload. You will need to upload a CSV with the first line being:
Assessment, Default, Minimum, Maximum

All subsequent lines represent individual assessments with a name, default weight, minimum weight, and maximum weight. You may use the below example as a template:
Quizzes, 25, 0, 100
Midterms, 25, 0, 100
Final, 50, 0, 100
